Where are more Cows?

Happy World Milk Day!!

Posted on June 1st, 2017

On the occasion of World Milk Day and cow being worshiped in my country, I was curious to know where more cows resided in the world.

This led me to search for a dataset from the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations website "www.fao.org".

Which country do you think has more cows?

So, it turns out that cows reside more in India, followed by China! ( Cows are competing with us!! ;) )

Statistics say that, in the year of 2014, total milk animals around the world were 765,916,303. Along with the cow, the statistics include buffalo, camel, goat and sheep as milk animals.

India, the most milk producing country ?
